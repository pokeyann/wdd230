Harvest Rocket City Chamber of Commerce Membership Plans

Our membership plans are designed to cater to the diverse needs and interests of businesses within our amazing community.

Non-Profit Membership - Free

  • Listing in the Chamber's online directory
  • Access to Chamber-sponsored events and networking opprotunities
  • Subscription to the Chamber's newsletter
  • Voting rights in Chamber elections

Bronze Membership - $250/year

  • All benefits of the Non-Profit Membership, plus:
  • Priority listing in the Chamber's online directory
  • Exclusive access to Chamber workshops and educational seminars
  • One free advertisement placement in the Chamber's newsletter annually
  • Discounted rates for Chamber-sponsored advertising opportunities

Silver Membership - $500/year

  • All benefits of the Bronze Membership, plus:
  • Enhanced listing with logo placement in the Chamber's online directory
  • Invitations to VIP networking events with community leaders and business owners
  • Two free advertisement placements in the Chamber's newsletter annually
  • Recognition as a Silver Member in Chamber publications and events

Gold Membership - $1000/year

  • All benefits of the Silver Membership, plus:
  • Premier placement with logo and description in the Chamber's online directory
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities for Chamber events and programs
  • Three free advertisement placements in the Chamber's newsletter annually
  • Special recognition as a Gold Member at Chamber events and in promotional materials
